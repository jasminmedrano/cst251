Lab 12:Advance Block-level Styling 2

The purpose of this lab was to learn more about styling blocks and seeing how centering some items in our files create a organized look.

Challenges

Some of my challenges were understanding how to float or where to float certian things without ruining my structure in my code.

Problems

Some of the bigger problems I had was making everything fall into place. Especially, with the float divs to go to the right or left. It made everything look wonky. After some experimenting I got it to look some what decent.

Results

I guess people weren't lying that float divs are a pain in the ass. They look so great in other websites but as soon as you try it. It makes you want to cry in the corner of your room. I did enjoy learning more about styling blocks elements.
